Trash and its effects on society

Bay Area-- All of the things that we know are being stocked with trash. We now see San Francisco and other major cities being flooded with trash. This has to stop.
Animals have been dying because of the about of toxins that trash produced when it isn't being thrown away.

So what can you do:

Put every piece of your trash in the place where it belongs.
  • Paper with paper
  • Glass with glass
  • Plastics with plastics
  • Metals with metals

If you see trash on the floor do the world a favor a pick it up. Help out with Community Service Hours to help create a safer environment for us and the animals.
